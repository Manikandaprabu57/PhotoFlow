Problem Definition

To design an intelligent event photo management system that:

• Automatically identifies and matches guest faces in bulk event photos using AI-powered Computer Vision

• Selects the best-matched photos for each guest using deep learning-based facial recognition algorithms

• Offers personalized photo collection recommendations based on face matching confidence and accuracy metrics

• Ensures secure and authenticated access to guest photos through advanced encryption and JWT-based authentication

• Automates the entire photo delivery workflow from upload to guest email notification

• Provides real-time analytics and insights on photo distribution, guest engagement, and processing efficiency

• Scales seamlessly across multiple simultaneous events with cloud-based infrastructure

• Eliminates manual photo sorting and organizational overhead through intelligent automation

• Enables photographers to manage their business globally with minimal operational resources

• Ensures data privacy and fraud prevention through comprehensive security and access control mechanisms
